# Alexandra Cárdenas
## A live coding presentation by Isaac P
- Alexandra is a composer/ live coder from Bogota, Colombia who has been live coding in Super Colider and Tidal cycles after being exposed to the ideas of live coding at National Center for the Arts in Mexico City
- What really stood out to me about Alexandra was her use of sample manipulation within tidal cycles to create huge and interesting walls of sound
- This is a quick example of some of the sample manipulation and her style of live coding
